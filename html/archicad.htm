Due to Graphisoft policy, Revizto plug-in is not automatically installed on ArchiCad. To install it, do the following:

1.Launch ArchiCad.

2.Navigate to Options > Add-on Manager.

3.Click the Search for More Add-Ons button.

4.Choose Revizto working folder and choose the relevant plug-in version in it.

ArchiCad stores links to plug-in, i.e. when Revizto is updated, no further action has to be taken (unless you move the working folder).
