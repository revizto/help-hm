Newly created projects are listed on the Manage Projccts page of the workspace web GUI (available to the License Owner and Administrators) with an Active or Inacteve status; a project is license is issued. Projects are active when there are active members working on them. Inactive projects do have members, but these are inactive (e.g. not yet activated their licenses).
