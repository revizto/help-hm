Revizto is designed for team work in Windows OS, but if you want to share a model with someone outside your team (i.e. not having a license) or using a different platform, you can export your model to .exe or for MAC. Note that exported files will not has issue management and editing functionality available in Revizto.

Export to EXE and for MAC

The Project menu allows you to export the selected project as .exe file or for MAC. The idea behind these options is sharing Revizto projects with people outside your license. Exported files can be open by Revizto application and contain all original elements, yet they have no collaboration and management options. Issues  will not be displayed.

Also export functionality may be a way out of you need to meet strict security requirements and may not upload your projects to the Cloud.
